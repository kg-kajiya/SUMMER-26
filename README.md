# SUMMER-26
10 week engineering skill personal development w/ focus on FSAE/automotive applications

---

## Goals

- Strengthen knowledge in suspension, chassis, engine, and powertrain design
- Develop practical skills through modeling, analysis, and applied projects
- Build a portfolio of CAD models, FEA reports, and design notes
- Track weekly progress, challenges, and insights

---

## Tools & References

- **CAD:** Siemens NX
- **FEA:** NX Simulation
- **References:**
  - *Race Car Vehicle Dynamics* — Milliken & Milliken
  - *Chassis Design: Principles and Analysis* — Milliken & Milliken (Costin & Phipps)
  - Hibbeler, *Mechanics of Materials* (supplemental refresher)
  - FSAE IC Rules Document

## TENTATIVE 10 WK PLAN OVERVIEW

| Week | Dates | Focus Area | Key Activities |
|------|-------|------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Jul 4 – Jul 11  | Mechanics of Materials refresher | Stress/strain, bending, torsion, safety factors; begin Milliken suspension geometry chapters |
| 2 | Jul 11 – Jul 18 | Mechanics + Vehicle Dynamics | Finish core mechanics review; continue Milliken (roll center, camber gain); sketch candidate component |
| 3 | Jul 18 – Jul 25 | Load case analysis | Hand-calculate cornering/braking/bump loads for chosen suspension component |
| 4 | Jul 25 – Aug 1  | CAD modeling in NX | Model suspension component (A-arm or upright) with proper dimensions/tolerancing|
| 5 | Aug 1 – Aug 8   | FEA setup + initial pass | Set up FEA study using hand-calc loads; check mesh convergence |
| 6 | Aug 8 – Aug 15  | FEA refinement + fatigue basics | Compare hand-calc vs. FEA stress; study S-N curves; basic fatigue check 
| 7 | Aug 15 – Aug 22 | Chassis frame section modeling | Model simplified space-frame section in NX per Milliken Chassis Design |
| 8 | Aug 22 – Aug 29 | Torsional stiffness FEA | Run torsional stiffness study on frame section; document assumptions/results |
| 9 | Aug 29 – Sep 5  | Powertrain literacy + mount bracket | Read FSAE IC packaging/driveline constraints; sketch/model engine mount bracket |
| 10 | Sep 5 – Sep 12 | Portfolio polish + sheet metal catch-up | Clean renders/drawings, short writeups per project; NX sheet metal practice |
